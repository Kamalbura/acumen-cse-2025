About ACUMEN

Discover what makes our tech fest the most anticipated event of the year

Celebrating Technology and Innovation

ACUMEN 2025 is Vasavi College of Engineering's flagship technical symposium hosted by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. Bringing together brilliant minds from across the country, ACUMEN offers a platform for students to showcase their technical prowess and innovative thinking.

With a wide range of events spanning coding competitions, hackathons, technical paper presentations, and gaming tournaments, ACUMEN caters to all kinds of tech enthusiasts. The event features cutting-edge workshops, expert talks, and exciting prizes for winners.
