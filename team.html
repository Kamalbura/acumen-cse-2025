Message from the HOD

Dr. T Aadilakshmi

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to ACUMEN 2025, the flagship technical event hosted by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Vasavi College of Engineering.

In today's rapidly evolving technological landscape, events like ACUMEN play a crucial role in nurturing innovation, fostering collaboration, and showcasing the incredible talent of our students. This platform serves as a bridge between academic learning and practical implementation, enabling participants to explore cutting-edge technologies and solve real-world challenges.

I extend my heartfelt appreciation to the faculty coordinators and student organizing team for their dedication and hard work in making this event possible. To all participants, I encourage you to make the most of this opportunity to learn, compete, and grow.

Dr. T Aadilakshmi
