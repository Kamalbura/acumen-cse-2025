About the Event

ACUMEN 2025 brings you the ultimate Free Fire Tournament where gamers can showcase their skills, strategy, and team coordination in one of the world's most popular battle royale mobile games. Compete in squad matches against other teams to be the last one standing and claim glory!

This high-octane tournament will test your reflexes, tactical decision-making, and teamwork as you navigate through intense gameplay situations. Whether you're a casual player or a serious gamer, the Free Fire Tournament offers an exciting competitive environment for all skill levels.

Tournament Format & Rules

Tournament Format

  • The tournament will follow a multi-round elimination format.
  • Preliminary Round: Teams will be divided into groups. Top teams from each group will advance.
  • Quarter-finals: Best of 2 matches with cumulative points.
  • Semi-finals: Best of 3 matches with cumulative points.
  • Finals: Best of 5 matches with cumulative points.

Game Settings

  • Game Mode: Battle Royale (Classic)
  • Map Rotation: Bermuda, Kalahari, Purgatory
  • Team Size: 4 players (Squad)
  • Match Duration: Approximately 20 minutes per game
  • Custom Room Settings: Standard tournament settings

Scoring System

Position Points

1st: 20 pts | 2nd: 14 pts | 3rd: 10 pts | 4th: 8 pts | 5th: 6 pts | 6th-12th: 4-1 pts

Kill Points

1 point per kill for each team member

Booyah Bonus

5 additional points for winning the match

Tie-breakers

Total kills, then highest placement in last match

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need to bring our own devices?
Yes, all participants must bring their own mobile devices (phones/tablets) with Free Fire installed. Make sure your devices are fully charged or bring power banks.
Can we have substitute players?
Yes, each team can register one substitute player. However, substitutions can only be made between matches, not during a match.
What happens if a player disconnects during a match?
The match will continue regardless of disconnections. We recommend ensuring a stable internet connection. Teams are responsible for their own connectivity. The venue will provide Wi-Fi, but backup options are recommended.
Are emulators allowed?
No, all players must play on mobile devices (smartphones or tablets). Use of emulators is strictly prohibited and will result in disqualification.
