About the Event

The ACUMEN 2025 Treasure Hunt is an exciting adventure that combines physical exploration, logical reasoning, and technical knowledge. Teams will navigate across the campus, following cryptic clues, solving technical and non-technical puzzles, and decoding riddles to find hidden locations and treasures.

This event tests your problem-solving abilities, teamwork, and quick thinking under time pressure. From computer science trivia to creative challenges, the Treasure Hunt offers a perfect blend of fun and intellectual stimulation that will keep you engaged throughout the journey to find the ultimate treasure!

Event Format

Hunt Structure

  • The hunt consists of multiple checkpoints spread across the campus.
  • Teams receive their first clue at the starting point.
  • Each checkpoint requires solving a puzzle or challenge to receive the clue for the next location.
  • Teams must follow the sequence of clues to reach the final treasure.
  • The first team to find the treasure and reach the final checkpoint wins.

Rules

  • All team members must stay together throughout the hunt.
  • Teams must take photos at each checkpoint as proof of completion.
  • Use of personal vehicles is not allowed.
  • Internet use is permitted for solving puzzles.
  • Interference with other teams is strictly prohibited and will result in disqualification.
